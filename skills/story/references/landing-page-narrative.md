# Landing Page Narrative

1. Hook: what this is and why it matters now.
2. Problem: what friction or cost the user recognizes.
3. Value proposition: the specific better state.
4. Proof: why users should believe it.
5. Features: how the product creates the value.
6. Objections: price, effort, risk, compatibility, trust.
7. CTA: next step with clear expectation.
