# Section Patterns

- Hero: product promise, primary action, credibility cue.
- Problem: current pain, cost, or missed opportunity.
- Value: concrete transformation, not generic benefit copy.
- Proof: logos, metrics, screenshots, quotes, or concrete artifacts.
- Features: grouped by user job rather than internal taxonomy.
- Pricing: decision support, not only plan cards.
- FAQ: objections and operational details.
- Footer: navigation, trust, and next step.
